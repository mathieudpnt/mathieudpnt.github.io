    • About me

    Bycatch of marine mammals by fishing gear is known to professionals, but few data are available. This lack of knowledge makes it very difficult to implement effective technical solutions adapted to dolphin behavior. The objective of the APOCADO project is to acquire knowledge about this interaction. In this context, I am performing various tasks to better understand the behavior of marine mammals near nets.
